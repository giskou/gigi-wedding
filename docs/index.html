Our Story

Giota and Giannis met under some quite unexpected circumstances in Gothenburg, Sweden, back in 2015.

He was living in Oslo, Norway. She was living in Freiburg, Germany. Even though Giota was not very impressed initially, Giannis' dedication made her reconsider. Their first year was mostly in remote, with Giannis visiting quite often. As things started to get more serious, Giannis convinced Giota to move to Oslo, which happened in late 2016. In December 2020, right before the pandemic was a thing, Giannis asked the question and Giota said yes!

Fast forward some global lockdowns, pregnancies and civil weddings, we are now happy to invite you to the official "Greek" wedding.
